Conventional Deadlift

warning High Risk
Sets x Reps 4 x 8

Quick Fix Tip Engage your lats by "pulling" the bar into your shins before the lift starts. Keep a neutral spine to avoid lumbar strain.

Weighted Pull-Ups

Sets x Reps 3 x Max
Rest 90s

Focus on driving the elbows down and back. Squeeze the shoulder blades at the top of the movement for maximum lat activation.

Military Press

Sets x Reps 4 x 10
Rest 60s

Execution Key Keep your core tight and glutes squeezed to prevent arching your lower back as the weight goes overhead.
